Privacy Policy for SkirmBot

SkirmBot does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All interactions with SkirmBot take place within Discord and are limited to necessary functionality, such as responding to commands and managing scrim announcements.

No user data is stored outside of Discord except for technical data needed to operate the bot (e.g., scrim listings), and this data is not shared with any third parties.

By using SkirmBot, you consent to this policy.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact the bot owner on Discord.
